About Membership

Registration is done once per semester. First time registration fees is Ksh. 100 , and for continuing members registration is Ksh. 50 per semester. We also accept Donation under this category to help us finance and obtain resources for mega projects per semester and organizing for special meetings. New members register below by filling the form and payment information will be provided once we contact you.

  • Reasons for Registration
  • Freely participate in weekly events
  • Be able to participate in AGGES competition
  • Registered members can vie for leadership position
  • Registration fees is used for financing club's activities
