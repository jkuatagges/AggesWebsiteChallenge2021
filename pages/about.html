About Us

AGGES: Who are we?

Association of Geospatial Engineering Students  is among the clubs at JKUAT.  The  club consist of like minded passionate Geomatic Engineering, Geospatial Engineering, and GIS students, experts in these field (including Alumni). AGGES was founded by the department of GEGIS in year 2005 by our Alumni team. AGGES is therefore meant to unleash the creativity of it's members and ignite super compatibility with tech skills.

AGGES Vision

Our mission is to provide the link between class work and industry skills throughout for our club members

AGGES Mission

Equip members with hands on experience with the current GIS technologies and provide better link to market skills.

Core Values

  • Vibrant spirit of comradeship and frientship
  • Passion for Exellence
  • Team Work
  • Integrity

History: Date of Founding to Date

Everything has it's own origin. We clone this from nature. We therefore can trace The Origin, founding and progress of AGGES club to date. Association of Geomatic Engineering students was found in the year 2005 by some Lecturers in GEGIS department including Dr. Felix Mutua, and other Alumni team. The club was registered in 2007 and was fully recognized as a club at JKUAT.  Under AGGES, we have another branch called   YOUTH MAPPERS which came to existence and is still under development . Up to date AGGES is still operational with membership from GEGIS department at first priority then other passionate members can   register
